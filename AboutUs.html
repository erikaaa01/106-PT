Our Story

At TropiStay, we are dedicated to providing you with an unforgettable stay in the heart of Panglao, Bohol. As an online hotel booking platform, we bring together a selection of the finest hotels, ensuring your getaway is both comfortable and unforgettable. Our journey began with the simple idea of offering travelers a seamless booking experience.

Over the years, we’ve expanded our platform to offer a diverse range of accommodations, from cozy boutique hotels to luxurious resorts. Whether you're here for a romantic retreat, a family vacation, or a solo adventure, TropiStay is your trusted partner in finding the perfect place to stay.
